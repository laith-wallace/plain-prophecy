# 3-Month SEO Strategy: Blog Publishing Recommendation

To achieve **significant SEO impact** for [Plain Prophecy](https://plainprophecy.com) over the next 3 months, you need a strategy that balances volume with topical authority.

## Current Context
- **Niche:** Christ-centred biblical prophecy, Historicist framework.
- **Platform:** Next.js + Convex (SEO-ready with sitemaps and metadata).
- **Goal:** Significant impact in 90 days.

---

## The Recommendation

For a site in this niche, "significant impact" means moving from being unranked to appearing for specific long-tail queries (e.g., "historicist vs futurist revelation") and building enough "Topical Authority" for Google to trust your deeper studies.

### Frequency Tiers

| Tier | Frequency | Total (3 Mos) | Expected Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Maintenance** | 1 post / week | 12 posts | Static or very slow growth. Good for keeping the site "fresh." |
| **Growth (Recommended)** | **2-3 posts / week** | **24–36 posts** | **Significant.** This is the "sweet spot" for building authority in 90 days. |
| **Aggressive** | 5 posts / week | 60 posts | **Maximum.** High risk of lower quality, but fastest way to dominate a niche. |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Consistency > Frequency.** 
> It is better to publish **2 posts every week** than to publish 10 in one week and zero for the rest of the month. Google's crawlers reward predictable activity.

---

## 3-Month Content Roadmap

### Month 1: Foundation & "What is?" Keywords
Focus on defining your unique perspective (Historicist, Christ-centred). 
- **Target:** "What is the historicist view of Revelation?" / "How is Jesus in the book of Daniel?"
- **Goal:** Establish the core vocabulary of your site.

### Month 2: Comparison & Objection Handling
Address common questions or conflicts in your niche.
- **Target:** "Futurism vs Historicism" / "Common misconceptions about the Antichrist."
- **Goal:** Capture traffic from users searching for alternatives to mainstream views.

### Month 3: Deep Dives & Semantic Interlinking
Create long-form "Pillar" content (2,000+ words) that links to your existing Study Lessons.
- **Target:** "Comprehensive Guide to the 70 Weeks of Daniel."
- **Goal:** Convert "searchers" into "students" by directing them to your `app/studies` content.

---

## SEO Checklist for Every Post
- [ ] **Length:** Aim for 1,200 – 1,800 words (higher for pillars).
- [ ] **Meta Tags:** Ensure `metaTitle` and `metaDescription` are filled in Convex.
- [ ] **Internal Linking:** Every blog post should link to at least one **Study Lesson** or **Doctrine** page.
- [ ] **External Linking:** Link to at least 2 high-authority external sources (e.g., Bible Gateway, academic historical sites).
- [ ] **Images:** Use descriptive ALT text for cover images.

---

## Summary for the Next 3 Months
To see a measurable shift in rankings and traffic by month 3, aim for **30 high-quality posts** (approx. 10 per month). This volume, combined with your existing high-quality study materials, will signal to search engines that Plain Prophecy is a primary authority on Christ-centred prophecy.
